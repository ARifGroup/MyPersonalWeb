How I'm Learning and Some Tips

I started learning coding after getting married in 2021 and I felt happy while learning coding at that time. I faced difficulties in learning because I had to work, take care of my family, and manage the household, as my wife was still relatively young in managing the household. Until I found a way to continue learning alongside my daily activities. The things I did are included in the tips I will share below.

This is tips from me that want to learn new thing and don't have time.

  1. Make sure you want to learn it and know how to use it. Something that didn't use is useless.
  2. Learn whereaver you can, and make sure you understand. Dont learn higher level quckly.
  3. Learn from that you like, if you like audio and then learn from youtube, if you like read then learn from pdf file or blog. In this era you can find anyting if you want search it.
  4. Use it in your life. For your work, for someone, or maybe just for share with your friend.

Thats all my learning routine everyday. Like humans in general, there are times when I feel lazy to study for a while.
